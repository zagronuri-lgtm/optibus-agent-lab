# Post-Run Comparison

## Purpose

Compare baseline evidence to approved scenario outputs. In v1, scenario outputs are supplied or simulated; they are not produced by a real Optibus Run.

## Required sections

- Baseline KPI facts.
- Scenario KPI facts and source labels.
- Deltas.
- Assumptions about compatibility.
- Risks from missing or simulated data.
- Recommendations for human review.

## Exit criteria

- The report clearly labels simulated values.
- No operational recommendation is presented as an observed fact.
